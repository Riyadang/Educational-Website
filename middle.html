Middle Wing


Our Middle Wing is one of the most important steps in the ladder of progress. Well planned study module amalgamated with dynamic co-curricular activities develop in our children sound academic skills and instill in them respect for perseverance, creativity and independent thinking. Emphasis is laid on confidence and character building, articulation of thoughts, problem solving skills, communication skills and resourcefulness. Children at this age undergo physical and emotional changes and we ensure that we provide them a supportive, safe, inviting and a joyful environment that not only promotes in-depth learning but also enhances their emotional well-being. This ensure that they are ready to confront challenges and complexities with panache and wisdom.
